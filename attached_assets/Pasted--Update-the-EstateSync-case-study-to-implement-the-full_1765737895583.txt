Checkpoint 01: Hidden Constraint
Content:
'A high-volume business was losing opportunities because matching records manually was slow and error-prone. New data would come in, someone would manually search existing records, spend hours filtering, and by then the opportunity was gone. The real cost: missed revenue, lost market share, and teams working insane hours just to stay competitive.
What was actually broken:
Matching took 2–4 hours per new record (manual database search + filtering).
Outreach was templated and impersonal (low response rates, ~8%).
Follow-ups relied on human memory (many leads/opportunities fell through cracks).
Zero visibility into which new records matched which existing records.
No audit trail (couldn't prove who matched what or why).
Escalations were manual and chaotic.
Sound familiar?
This applies whether you're matching:
Properties to clients
Customers to product recommendations
Leads to sales reps
Orders to inventory
Job candidates to open positions'
Checkpoint 02: Discovery
Content:
'During the audit, we mapped the entire workflow: data intake → manual search → decision → outreach → follow-up → conversion. We discovered that the team was spending 60% of their time on activities that could be automated, and their biggest fear was losing personal touch (we solved that by keeping human judgment in every decision).
Key insight:
The bottleneck wasn't complexity—it was speed. They needed to match records in minutes, not hours, and send personalized outreach automatically while they focused on closing/conversion.
The audit revealed:
Average time to match a record: 2–4 hours (mostly waiting/searching).
Matching accuracy: ~70% (human error, fatigue).
Records lost in backlog: ~15% per week (never got matched).
Outreach response rate: ~8% (templated, impersonal).
Follow-up consistency: ~40% (depends on who remembers).'
Checkpoint 03: Blueprint
Content:
'System architecture (kept intentionally generic):
Intelligent Matching Engine
Learns from your existing data (what successful matches look like).
Real-time matching of new records against stored profiles/criteria.
Ranks matches by probability/relevance.
Flags anomalies and edge cases for human review.
Personalized Outreach Generator
AI-generates contextual messages based on match quality and user history.
Messages are customizable by humans before sending (no auto-send).
Maintains brand voice and personal touch.
Tracks every message sent (audit trail).
Unified Command Dashboard
One place to see all matched records, outreach status, follow-up reminders.
Real-time visibility (what's pending, what's in progress, what's done).
Customizable views (by team, by priority, by stage).
Human-in-the-Loop Approval System
AI suggests matches and outreach; humans approve before action.
Escalation logic for uncertain matches.
Complete audit trail (who approved what, when, why).
Integration Layer
Connects to your existing systems (data sources, CRM, communication tools).
Custom connectors for your specific data format.
Key decisions:
Matching runs in real-time so teams see opportunities as they happen.
Messages are generated by AI but approved by humans (maintains control + personal brand).
Dashboard shows only actionable items (no noise, pure signal).
Every action is logged (compliance + accountability).'
